The Wedding

Jon & Michelle were married at the Codman Estate in Lincoln, Massachusetts on October 13, 2018.

Our Story

First kiss, last kiss

Michelle and Jon met at Camp Calumet Lutheran as teenagers and became friends. They later shared what turned out to be both of their first kisses. Sadly, as the years flew by, they lost touch throughout college and beyond. But two years ago, Michelle discovered that she and Jon shared a mutual friend. Michelle asked Jon to lunch. They had a lovely date and picked up where they left off. Over the past two years, Michelle and Jon have grown closer on trips to St. John, Chicago, and California. Weather permitting, they fish at Spot Pond, sail in Boston Harbor, and enjoy nature. They're ready to start the next chapter in their lives, joined in marriage.

The engagement


The flutter of snowflakes. A waterfall backdrop. A chilly December day. Jon proposed at the Basin at Franconia Notch.

Michelle said yes.
